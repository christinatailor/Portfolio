Item Catalog

I will develop an application that provides a list items within a variety of categories as well as provide a user registration and authentication system. Registered users will have the ability to post, edit and delete their own items.

Users will be able to list music supplies, acting supplies, sewing Supplies, craft Supplies, and art supplies. I chose these categories for the website because I love the Arts. I used to act in high school. I love crafting as well. I have knitting equipment, a sewing machine, and an overlocker machine. So, I've decided to build something for crafters. It is more for sharing inventory and taking inventory. Not for buying.
